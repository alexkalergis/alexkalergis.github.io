About Me

Graduate Electrical and Computer Engineer with a passion for merging design thinking with human-computer interaction, AI, and software engineering to solve challenging problems and create meaningful user experiences. My journey spans hands-on experience in machine learning, product development, and managing digital projects for both startups and family-run businesses. I've contributed to the creation of ML products from ideation to MVP stages, honed skills in frontend development, and explored UX/UI design across various fields, including gaming and design systems.

Beyond engineering, I'm driven by creativity and believe in blending the technical and artistic to create solutions that matter. From building reinforcement learning models for robotic tasks to managing the digital presence of a family-owned coffee bar, I thrive at the intersection of technology, design, and human connection. In pursuit of continuous growth, I seek opportunities that challenge and inspire me to make an impact on the way humans and technology interact.

When not coding or designing, you’ll find me exploring the outdoors, diving into extreme sports, seeking minimalism, or enjoying deep conversations over a good cup of coffee.
